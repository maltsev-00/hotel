About our hotel

The MLK hotel has been operating since 2015. Boutique hotel in the quiet center of Minsk - since 2018 The cat rooms are made of toughened thick glass and each is equipped with a security camera. Our pet hotel has the opportunity to benefit

Our clinic employs specialists with veterinary education. In the event of an illness, the pet will be able to quickly provide the necessary assistance.
